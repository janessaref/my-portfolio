About Me

I graduated with a bachelor's degree in management of financial institutions in 2017 at De La Salle University - Manila. I am a dancer who was a part of the official dance organization of the university known as La Salle Dance Company - Street. I took up leadership roles in the organization as the division manager for finance in 2015-2016 and division manager of marketing in 2016-2017.

I'm a competitive person. My sport is badminton. I am into fashion and I also love music. The instruments I play are the piano and the ukulele. I enjoy karaoke. I am adventurous and love amusement parks. I love animals. I love to travel and eat.
